Help & Support

Precision answers, guided workflows, and secure help for your Hurly operations.

Export Reports Go to Payroll

Getting Started

Add employees Go to Employees and click Add Employee to create staff profiles.
Record attendance In Attendance, use Add Worker, Roll Call, or Clock In/Out.
Create payroll Open Payroll, click Create Payroll Run, choose tasks, days, and status.
Export reports Go to Reports, filter by date, then download a PDF summary.

Quick Help

Payroll PDF not downloading? Refresh the page and try again. Offline mode blocks exports.
Missing payroll runs in Reports? Clear the filter or expand the date range.
Need to edit a site? Go to Settings, open Work Sites, and click Edit.
