Help & Support

Quick answers, guides, and contact details.

Export Reports Go to Payroll

Getting Started

Add employees
Go to Employees and click Add Employee to create staff profiles.
Record attendance
In Attendance, use Add Worker, Roll Call, or Clock In/Out.
Create payroll
Open Payroll, click Create Payroll Run, choose tasks, days, and status.
Export reports
Go to Reports, filter by date, then download PDF.

Quick Help

Payroll PDF not downloading?
Refresh the page and try again. If offline, PDF export may be blocked.
Missing payroll runs in Reports?
Clear the filter or expand the date range.
Need to edit a site?
Go to Settings, open Work Sites, and click Edit.
